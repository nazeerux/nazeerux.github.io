The Problem

How might we design a simple digital solution that allows elderly individuals, people with disabilities, and those living abroad to easily access and manage ration services without needing to physically visit ration shops?:

The Solution

To address this problem, Ezy Ration provides a simple and accessible mobile platform that allows users to manage ration services digitally. The app enables users to view their digital ration card, track monthly ration availability, and monitor transaction history without visiting the ration shop. It also allows family members who are abroad to check and manage ration benefits for their households remotely. By offering clear information, notifications, and an easy-to-use interface, the app helps reduce the need for physical visits and makes ration services more accessible for elderly and disabled users.
